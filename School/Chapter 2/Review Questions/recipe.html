Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Pasta
  • Pasta Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Put Pasta in water
  3. Heat up Pasta Sauce
  4. (Optional) Add seasoning to the sauce
  5. Ensure pasta noodles are done
  6. Drain water in strainer
  7. Ensure pasta sauce is hot
  8. Mix the pasta noodles and pasta sauce
  9. FEED
